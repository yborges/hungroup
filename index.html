About US

We are ADAM & AOKI,

One of the first companies to confront functional clothing.

We are proud to represent what the future of smart clothing will look like.

Using innovative technologies together with the latest fashion trends, we create products that are both functional to the wearer, but also reflect the modern fashion trends.

Our first product, the The Smart Shirt is able to warn you when you are in danger of being sunburnt.

We managed to achieve this using our signature Smart Fabric which changes color under specific conditions.

Combined with the color changing smart fabric, we have our SmartShirt App, which notifies you when you have spent too much time in the sun, without proper protection.

This is what we have managed to achieve thus far, and we are looking forward to doing more good in the future!

We believe that together, we can create truly functional and stylish clothing!
